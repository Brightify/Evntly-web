Frequently Asked Questions (FAQS)

How much does it cost?

Evntly is free to use. Every artist sets their custom price or hourly price , so you can always find the price you are looking for. Each event has a set duration and the artist is paid beforehand, including a small service fee.

How can I register as a professional?

It's easy! During sign up there is an option to create a professional account. You then set up your professional profile and connect your Stripe account for payouts. You can create a new account right in the app.

Can I cancel my payments?

If, for any reason, you do not want the requested professional and already paid or you decided to cancel your event, you can always contact our support and we will help you accordingly.

What should I do if the professional does not come?

Write to our support as soon as possible. We will return your payment and contact the professional to ensure that such incovenience would not happen ever again.

What happens to my money when I pay the professional?

We hold onto funds a few days after the professional completes his/her job, so you don’t have to worry!
